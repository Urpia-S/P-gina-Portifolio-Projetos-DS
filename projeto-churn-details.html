Deep Dive into Customer Retention

In an increasingly competitive digital landscape, retaining existing users is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project addresses the critical challenge of predicting which users are most likely to churn based on their historical behavior and demographic data.

By deploying advanced ensemble methods and deep learning architectures, we shifted the focus from reactive damage control to proactive customer success interventions.
